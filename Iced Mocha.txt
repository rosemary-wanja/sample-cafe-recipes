ICED MOCHA 

INGREDIENTS :
25cl of milk
Whipped cream
5 ice cubes
Café
Chocolat en poudre
White chocolate syrup
Topping Chocolate
 MATERIAL
Blender
Topping dispenser
Whipped cream dispenser
PREPARATION
Start by making an espresso with your coffee machine
Put the ice cubes, the espresso, the milk, a tablespoon of chocolate powder and two tablespoons of white chocolate syrup into the blender
Blend them together
Decorate the inside of your glass with drizzles of chocolate coulis
Pour the mix into your glass
Bury your drink under whipped cream
Drizzle more chocolate coulis over the whipped cream, and add a pinch of ground coffee
Last but not least, enjoy your delicious drink!
