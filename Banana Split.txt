Banana Split

Tastes just like an old fashioned banana split blended.

Ingredients:
5 oz. Dr. Smoothie Strawberry Banana Concentrate
3 oz. Milk
1 (50cc) scoop Cafe Essentials Chocoholics Choice
14 - 15 oz. Cup of Ice

Directions:
Add the ingredients in the order listed and blend.
Yield:
1 - 16 oz. Beverage
